Project Overview

Tourism Office Sales & Performance Report (September 2025)

This project serves as a complete case study in sales and performance analysis within the tourism sector. The primary goal was to convert raw Excel sales data into actionable business intelligence by creating two professional, interactive dashboards in Microsoft Power BI.

Key Achievements & Impact:

  • KPI Tracking: Calculated and visualized critical KPIs, including Total Sales ($880K) and Total Commissions ($39.66K).
  • Customer Profiling: Identified that the 21-30 age group generates the highest commission, providing a clear focus for marketing campaigns.
  • Profitability Analysis: Detailed breakdown of sales, cost, and commission by Booking Company, identifying Jeddah and Riyadh as the top destinations.
  • Operational Insights: Mapped commission earned per employee and tracked daily sales trends to identify peak performance periods.
  • Value Proposition: This report delivers a visual, data-backed tool that management can use to optimize pricing, improve employee efficiency, and enhance overall profitability.
